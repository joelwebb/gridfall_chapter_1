🎮 Game Help

🎯 How to Play

Drag and drop your player units to move them around the battlefield. Plan your strategy carefully!

  • Touch and drag any player unit (green circles) to move them
  • You have 4 seconds to make your move once you leave your starting position
  • Enemy units (red circles) will move automatically

⏱️ Timer System

The timer starts counting down when you move outside your current grid cell:

  • Timer shows remaining time in seconds
  • Make your move before time runs out
  • Timer resets after each turn

🎮 Controls

Game controls and interface elements:

  • ⏸️ Pause - Pause the game anytime
  • ❓ Help - Open this help menu
  • HP bars show unit health
  • Level indicator shows current stage

🗿 Terrain Elements

Watch out for special terrain that can affect your units:

  • Spikes - Deal damage when stepped on
  • Poison - Continuous damage over time
  • Some terrain may provide bonuses

💡 Strategy Tips

Master these tactics to become a better player:

  • Keep your units close together for support
  • Use terrain to your advantage
  • Plan multiple moves ahead
  • Protect wounded units behind healthy ones
  • Corner enemies when possible

⚔️ Combat Tips

Effective battle strategies:

  • Attack from multiple directions
  • Focus fire on single targets
  • Use ranged units from safe distances
  • Block enemy movement paths
